Tardigrade

Tardigrades can survive extreme temperatures, toxic environments, and even the vacuum of space.

  • Scientific Name: Tardigrada
  • Phylum: Tardigrada; Spallanzani, 1777
  • Rank: Phylum
  • Kingdom: Animalia

Scientists classify these micro-animals as extremophiles because they are capable of surviving in extreme conditions. While strangely cute, these tiny animals are almost indestructible. They can live for a decade without food or water.
